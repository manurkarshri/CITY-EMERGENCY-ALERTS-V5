# User Guide

## Home
Shows current city situation, weather, rain, traffic, flood, power and public safety overview.

## Alerts
Critical and actionable items such as official warnings, flood alerts, road closures, fires, power hazards and severe weather.

## Incidents
Local awareness feed for accidents, traffic blocks, theft/crime, civic disruption, waterlogging, tree fall and other nearby events.

## Official
Direct links to trusted government and agency sources.

## Emergency
Emergency phone numbers, nearby map shortcuts, shelters, hospitals, safety checklist and guidance.

Always call **112** for life-threatening emergencies.
