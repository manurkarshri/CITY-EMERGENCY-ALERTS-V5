# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

This project exists to improve public emergency awareness and community resilience.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Community members are encouraged to:

- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Focus on facts
- Share helpful suggestions
- Help improve public safety
- Respect different opinions

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Deliberate misinformation
- Malicious code
- Disruptive behaviour

---

## Emergency Information

Users should avoid posting unverified emergency information.

Whenever possible, official government or emergency service sources should be referenced.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate contributions and restrict participation where necessary to protect the community.

---

## Our Goal

CITY EMERGENCY ALERTS is intended to be a trusted community resource that helps people make informed decisions during emergencies.
